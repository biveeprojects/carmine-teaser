Gateway Academy is a curated learning platform offering both online and blended learning courses.

It will cultivate communities focused on financial inclusion in Sub-Saharan Africa.

Gateway Academy is partnering with leading training, content, and financial service providers to address training and knowledge gaps through relevant online and blended courses. These courses will augment and build upon existing financial inclusion training.

Partnering with CGAP on Gateway Academy has a number of benefits, including:

  • Addressing a diversity of training needs
  • Reaching a larger audience of entry- and mid-level staff
  • Improving training logistics and efficiencies
  • Reducing workplace time and opportunity costs
  • Accessing a community to share knowledge and ideas
  • Increasing brand recognition across the industry
  • Gaining expertise in developing online courses
